"Welcome to GoBikes"

GoBikes is community service provider. Providing cab services through bikes.
Cabs are essential part of urban life, especially for job going peoples.
We require cabs for going to work, malls, shops, restaurants, picnics, nad other travelling purposes.
In this era of growing fuel prices using public transport is always a better option.
But sometimes using cabs costs more than using personal vehical.
So the question arises, Do we really need high cost cabs or personal vehicals for travelling in cities?
The answer is of corse NO !!
GoBikes provides bike cab service that is beneficial for solo travelling and costs almost half than cabs or rickshaws.
